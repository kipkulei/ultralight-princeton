Flying Kite outside Spelman Hall

This is a video I took of my friend flying my kite on Monday 03/25 outside Spelman Hall. Being a generally calm day, the wind was not the strongest but rather came in small gusts lasting about five to ten seconds. This was enough to only keep the kite afloat for that long. On an otherwise windy day, the open area between Spelman and Pine Hall would be a good place to fly such a kite.
