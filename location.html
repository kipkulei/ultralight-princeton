A short trip to NYC

Taking the train to New York City before the end of my spring break proved to be extremely worth it. We found ourselves drawn to the lively atmosphere of an indoor mini-golf spot called the Swingers Crazy Golf - Nomad. Stepping inside, we were immediately greeted by the vibrant neon lights and upbeat music that filled the air, setting the stage for an unforgettable indoor mini-golf experience. As we made our way through the whimsical corridors, my senses were bombarded by a kaleidoscope of colors and sounds of good music by the DJ at the corner of the first room at the end of the corridor. We chose to do the Windmill course where each hole presented a new challenge. With every swing of the club, I could feel the excitement building, the satisfying thud of the ball against the turf sending a thrill through my fingertips. After an exhilarating round of mini-golf, where I tallied sencond in the group, we all ventured out into the bustling streets of lower Manhattan,my stomach rumbling in anticipation of a delicious early dinner. Following the tantalizing aroma of sizzling street food, we stumbled upon a cozy Mexican eatery tucked away amidst the busy streets. Inside, the warm glow of sunlight filtered through the windows, casting a golden hue over the eclectic decor. The air was filled with the mouthwatering scent of roasted beef and freshly brewed coffee, beckoning us to settle in and indulge. As I savored each bite of huevos rancheros and took in the lively chatter of fellow diners, I couldn't help but feel a sense of contentment wash over me. In that moment, surrounded by the vibrant energy of New York City, I was reminded of the joy that comes from exploring new places and indulging in the simple pleasures of life.
