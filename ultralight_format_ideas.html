My Ultralight Format Ideas

1. Daily Color Rhymes

Guide

  1. Once a day, select a random color of your choosing
  2. Start by noting down the phrase, Today I choose the color, ... and fill in your chosen color
  3. Add another line describing something that happened in your day, ensuring the last word rhymes with the color you chose
  4. Reflect on these rhymes every week to evaluate how your week's been. Feel free to use a color more than once but the second line has to be unique to the day

Examples

  • Today I choose the color blue, I am afraid I might have caught the flu.
  • Today I choose the color red, Without office hours I'll fail, I dread.

2. Vacation Destinations

Guide

  1. Once a day, pick a random vacation destination and upload a picture of it.
  2. List two reasons why you think it is a good place to spend a vacation.
  3. At the end of each week, pick your best location.
  4. Now you have a comprehensive list of potential vacation destinations.

Examples


  1. Day 1: Kenya
    • Safaris at Maasai Mara National Park.
    • White sandy beaches at Diani.



  2. Day 2: Fiji.
    • Snorkelling near the beaches of Menjangan island.
    • Surfing in Uluwatu and Canggu beach.
Week 1 Selection: Kenya.

3. Best Bars

Guide

  1. Select one song of your choosing each day.
  2. Upload the spotify cover image and note down the artist's name(s)
  3. Also note down the lyric that you like/ resonate most from the track

Examples


  1. J. Cole - Love Yourz
    "It's the beauty in the struggle, ugliness in the success"


  2. Ye - Only One
    "No, you're not perfect but you're not your mistakes"

References

  • Love Yourz, J.Cole
  • Only One - Ye West
